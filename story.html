story

A massive black hole of unkown origin suddenly appeared at the edge of the solar system and seemed to attract only and all manga-related energy available on Earth.

Unbearable anxiety arose as familiar manga characters and stories were transformed into splendid aurora rays devoured by the black hole.

WAIT. WHAT...IS...THAT?!

The energy, on its way to the black hole, was captured by a flaming planet, followed by FOMO stones emerging from the surface of the star one after another.

Is it the birth of an evil conspiracy? Or a new adventure about to unfold?
